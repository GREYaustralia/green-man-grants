THE JUDGING

Phase 1: All submissions will be evaluated by a judging panel made up of TAC and key road safety partners. All applicants will be notified whether they have been successful or not via email by 14 th October 2016.

Phase 2: Successful finalists will be required to present their idea to the judging panel - including industry experts. A detailed brief of what will be required will be outlined at this stage. Presentations will be scheduled from 31 st October, 2016. This presentation will require an overview of the concept including a detailed breakdown of the budget funds requested.

Phase 3: Ten winners will be announced on 14 th November, 2016 and will receive a budget of up to $50,000 to produce their idea. TAC and an industry mentor will work with you in creating your idea and manage the budget. A personal prize of $5,000 will be awarded to you when the idea is complete.
