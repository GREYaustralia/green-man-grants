ENTRY GUIDELINES

Each entry must meet the following criteria.

SUBMISSION CRITERIA

  • Entries to be received by 14th September 2016
  • Entry must be submitted via online form with all sections fully completed
  • Entries by an individual only (no groups entries allowed)
  • Entrant must not have had their driver's licence cancelled
  • Entrant must be aged between 18 and 25 years of age at the time of entry
  • Entrant must be a Victorian resident
  • Entrant must not have had their driver's licence cancelled

IDEA CRITERIA

Your idea must:

  • relate to the brief
  • be relevant to under 25s
  • be truly original
  • be innovative and should aim to change the way young people think about road safety
  • be able to be produced within your proposed budget (up to $50,000)
