Introduction

Entering Green Man Grants is pretty simple. There are three briefs for you to choose from. Just pick the one that inspires you the most and get cracking on your concept. You can enter your idea in whichever manner best portrays your concept. Either by uploading a video, audio, image or a text entry. It can be anything from an app to an event; but importantly, it must answer the brief.

Entering

At this initial stage, you will only need to provide a summary of your concept. To make sure that our judging panel understands and shares your vision, be sure to articulate the idea as much as possible.

Shortlist

If your idea is shortlisted, you will be invited to present to our panel. At this time you will be required to present a more developed outline including a detailed breakdown of the funds you are requesting (up to $50,000).

Winners

There will be a selection of winners across the three categories below. Each winner will be partnered with an industry expert, who will guide and mentor you through the process of bringing your idea to life.


Entries close at 5pm on 15th September 2016.
