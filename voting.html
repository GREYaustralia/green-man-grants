The Pedestrian's Choice

$1000 to be won

The Pedestrian's Choice is an additional prize, rewarding the idea that gets the most votes in the online gallery - the crowd favourite.

After moderation, each entry will be posted to the gallery where it can be viewed, voted on, and shared via URL directly to Facebook & Twitter, along with the #greenmangrants.

The entry that receives the most votes will win $1000 prize money! There will also be a random draw for a further $1000 prize for all of the people who have voted - so get sharing and voting!
